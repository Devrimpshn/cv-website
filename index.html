Projects

Time In

In this psychological puzzle game, the player wakes up in a ransacked house filled with clues to an apparent break-in. As they solve environmental puzzles and uncover hidden keys, the truth gradually unfolds. A blood-filled bathtub, a hidden knife, and a chilling voice recording lead to a disturbing realization — the player has murdered their spouse, but their mind refuses to accept it. In the final moment, the player must make a choice: turn themselves in, or destroy the evidence. The game ends with this haunting dilemma, leaving morality in the player's hands.

The Sicilia

Sicilia is a relentless 2D top-down shooter where bullets never stop flying. After getting entangled with a brutal mafia, you must fight your way through waves of enemies, assassinate powerful mob lieutenants, and face the ultimate crime boss. With intense combat, explosive encounters, and non-stop action, Sicilia delivers an adrenaline-fueled experience from start to finish.

Perpetrum

Perpretum is a narrative-driven psychological game that begins in the quiet, grief-filled room of the protagonist who is mourning the loss of a close friend. After falling asleep, the player finds themselves in a dreamlike maze shaped by avoidance and inner turmoil. As the journey progresses, memories resurface and the player explores a mysterious mansion with rooms reflecting different emotions such as anger and acceptance. By confronting these feelings, the player eventually returns to the original room, now transformed into a bright and joyful space. Perpretum explores themes of healing, emotional struggle, and personal growth.
